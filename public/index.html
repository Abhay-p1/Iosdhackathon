About Us

The second-largest country in the world, India, is home to approximately 63 million people of the Deaf and Dumb community. Although sign language has been evolving within the country for the last hundreds, it is still unknown to a large chunk of the population.

  • We present a web application where a person familiar with Indian Sign Language can easily communicate in real-time, with someone who is not!
  • You can now input your audio/text and this application will break it into keywords and demonstrate the same in sign language.
  • This application can even access your camera, identify your gestures and convert them into audio/text.
